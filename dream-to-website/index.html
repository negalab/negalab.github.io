Before the theory, before the tool, before the website — a dream you can see. Every speaker on this page wrote a single sentence about what they want to bring into the world, then turned that sentence into a Figma deck. By June 10, each of these dreams will be a working website. This is the first step of the journey — dream to website.

From a Dream to a Website

This page is the Web Design edition of the Dream Conference — a campus-wide series at SMIT where students across departments turn their dreams into class projects. Here, the medium is the web. The journey is DTDT: Dream → Theory → Design → Tool. Week 11 was the dream. Week 12 is the theory. On June 10, each of these speakers takes the floor and presents their own working website live. Final wrap-up on June 17.
