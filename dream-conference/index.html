From a Dream to a Website

This conference is part of DTDT — a four-week journey at SMIT: Dream → Theory → Design → Tool. Week 11 was the dream you can see. Week 12 is the foundation under it. Week 14 (June 10) is when each of these speakers takes the floor and presents their own site live. Final wrap-up on June 17.
